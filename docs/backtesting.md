## Modules

The backtesting package is composed of a few focused modules:

- `backtest_engine` ‚Äì glue code that wires together universe selection,
  alpha models, portfolio construction and execution into a Backtrader run.
- `base_strategy` ‚Äì common bookkeeping and helpers used by all provided
  strategies.
- `commission` ‚Äì cost models for simulating broker commissions and spreads.
- `indicators` ‚Äì reusable signal and technical indicator implementations.
- `optimization_engine` ‚Äì genetic‚Äëalgorithm tooling for parameter searches.
- `portfolio` ‚Äì utilities for combining the results of several strategies and
  producing performance reports.
- `strategies` ‚Äì ready‚Äëto‚Äëuse Backtrader `Strategy` subclasses.
- `universe` ‚Äì helpers for building trading universes (e.g. most liquid symbols).


`pwb_toolbox.backtesting.strategies` ships a collection of portfolio templates
with different rebalancing rules and signal expectations:

- **DailyEqualWeightPortfolio** ‚Äì holds all assets with a long signal and
  allocates equal weight each day.
- **DailyLeveragePortfolio** ‚Äì goes long with fixed leverage when the signal is
  1 and is otherwise flat.
- **EqualWeightEntryExitPortfolio** ‚Äì opens equally‚Äëweighted positions when an
  entry condition triggers and leaves existing winners untouched.
- **DynamicEqualWeightPortfolio** ‚Äì event‚Äëdriven equal‚Äëweight portfolio that can
  rebalance on any signal change or only when the set of long assets changes.
- **MonthlyLongShortPortfolio** ‚Äì once per month allocates half of the leverage
  to longs and half to shorts based on a universe‚Äëaware signal.
- **MonthlyLongShortQuantilePortfolio** ‚Äì monthly rebalance that ranks assets
  by a per‚Äëasset signal and goes long the strongest and short the weakest.
- **MonthlyRankedEqualWeightPortfolio** ‚Äì monthly equal‚Äëweight portfolio with an
  optional ranking step and support for keeping only the top *N* assets.
- **QuarterlyTopMomentumPortfolio** ‚Äì every quarter concentrates exposure in the
  single asset with the strongest recent momentum.
- **RollingSemesterLongShortPortfolio** ‚Äì semi‚Äëannual rebalancing template that
  accumulates long/short signals over six‚Äëmonth windows.
- **WeeklyLongShortDecilePortfolio** ‚Äì rebalances weekly and trades the top and
  bottom deciles of a signal distribution.
- **WeightedAllocationPortfolio** ‚Äì turns user‚Äëprovided weights into integer
  share positions under a leverage constraint.
